Overview

Why You Should Use All Three Together

By 2026, Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini have evolved well past simple chatbots into full agents. They read documents, research the web, manipulate spreadsheets directly, send emails, and even collaborate as teams of AI. Each tool has different strengths depending on the task, so combining all three situationally is, as of 2026, the most effective approach.

Playbook

Step-by-Step Playbooks by Scenario

Exactly which tool to use, in which order, and how many steps, across 10 common business scenarios.

01

Mid-to-long-term strategy

GeminiClaudeChatGPT
  • GeminiDeep Research (max-depth) to research industry trends and competitor movements over a 5-year horizon.
  • ClaudeProjects — upload the research alongside internal financials and ask for 3 scenarios (conservative/base/aggressive) with a SWOT.
  • ChatGPTCanvas — polish the draft into a 1-page executive summary for leadership.
  • ClaudeSkills — save as an "annual strategy report generator" workflow to reuse next year.
Example prompt (step 2, Claude)
We're building a mid-to-long-term strategy for 2027–2029 for our company (industry: ___, revenue scale: ___). Using the attached market research and financials, lay out 3 growth scenarios (conservative/base/aggressive) along with a SWOT and key risk factors.
02

Market analysis

GeminiChatGPTClaude
  • GeminiDeep Research for an initial pass on market size and growth rate.
  • ChatGPTDeep Research to cross-check the same topic from different sources (news, patents, reviews).
  • Claude — feed in both reports and ask it to "reconcile the overlaps and contradictions, and draw a final set of insights" into one conclusion.
03

Price analysis

ChatGPTClaudeGemini
  • ChatGPTfor Excel — paste in competitor pricing data and ask for a price-positioning matrix.
  • ClaudeCode — simulate cost structure and margin sensitivity in Python.
  • GeminiSlides — turn the results into a visual slide deck to share with stakeholders.
04

Product trend analysis

GeminiClaudeChatGPT
  • Geminiin Chrome agentic browsing — open several review/community tabs at once and auto-generate a comparison table.
  • ClaudeSkills — register as a "monthly product trend briefing" to run on a schedule.
  • ChatGPTCanvas — edit the final briefing document and format it for distribution.
05

Data analysis (general)

ChatGPTClaudeGemini
  • Structured spreadsheets: handle immediately with ChatGPTfor Excel or GeminiSheets integration.
  • Large or unstructured data (logs, multi-file merges): write and run Python scripts with ClaudeCode.
  • Turn results into a visual report with Gemini Slides or ChatGPT Canvas.
06

Process improvement

ClaudeGemini
  • ClaudeAgent Teams — split into "current-process analyst / redesign lead / risk reviewer" roles and review in parallel.
  • Register the finalized improvement as a ClaudeSkill so it becomes a repeatable standard process.
  • GeminiDocs — document it and share in real time across the department.
07

RPA (recurring downloads & web automation)

ChatGPTClaude
  • ChatGPTAgent Mode logs into a given site, downloads the data, and saves it automatically (for non-CAPTCHA sites).
  • ClaudeComputer Use organizes and renames the downloaded files into your folder structure.
  • Connect recurring jobs to a no-code tool like n8n for daily/weekly scheduling.
08

Automated emailing

GeminiChatGPT
  • Geminiin Meet summarizes the meeting and automatically emails attendees.
  • ChatGPTOutlook integration drafts personalized emails to customers in bulk.
  • Geminiin Gmail gives a final review before sending.
09

Executive reporting

GeminiClaudeChatGPT
  • Gemini or ChatGPT Deep Research to gather data and research.
  • ClaudeSkills' "monthly board report generator" auto-drafts the report against your template.
  • ChatGPTCanvas converts it to Word/PPT and polishes the final design.
Example prompt (step 2, running the Claude Skill)
Write the board report using this month's attached revenue, cost, and research data. Template: Summary → Performance highlights → Risks → Next month's action items.
10

Cross-department collaboration

ClaudeGemini
  • ClaudeAgent Teams splits work by department role (planning, dev, QA, etc.) with one shared task list to track progress.
  • GeminiDocs / Sheets for real-time co-editing and auto-shared meeting summaries.
  • Geminiin Chrome multi-tab view rolls up each department's status into one screen for a weekly briefing.
